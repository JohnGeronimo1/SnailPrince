ABOUT THE OSCARS

The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences (AMPAS) has opened the process of voting for Academy Awards to the world. After movies are nominated for each category, you can vote on who should win the award for each cateogry. The qualifications to vote are that they must have viewed a minimum of 5 Oscar Winning movies.
